About

Yuwei Liu is a tenure-track researcher at JASRI/SPring-8, currently responsible for XAFS experiments at Beamline BL14B2. His work focuses on user support, technical development, data analysis, and AI-assisted tools for synchrotron experiments.

With a background in materials chemistry, X-ray/neutron techniques, and signal/data processing, he aims to integrate chemistry, physics, and mathematics to help researchers create new scientific outcomes.

Research Interests

  • Data-driven analysis of synchrotron spectroscopy
  • Practical method development for synchrotron-based materials research
  • AI-assisted tools for measurement planning and research support
  • Interdisciplinary applications bridging chemistry, physics, and data science
