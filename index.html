About

Yuwei Liu is a tenure-track researcher at the Japan Synchrotron Radiation Research Institute (JASRI), SPring-8. His work focuses on X-ray absorption spectroscopy (XAFS), EXAFS analysis, in-situ XAFS instrumentation, and synchrotron-based characterization of multimetallic nanoparticles.

He is currently involved in user support and technical development at Beamline BL14B2, SPring-8, including transmission XAFS, fluorescence XAFS, sample absorption evaluation, beamline automation, and AI-assisted tools for XAFS experiments.

Research Interests

  • X-ray absorption spectroscopy (XAFS) and EXAFS analysis
  • In-situ and operando XAFS measurements
  • Synchrotron radiation instrumentation and beamline automation
  • Fluorescence and transmission XAFS at BL14B2, SPring-8
  • Multimetallic and high-entropy alloy nanoparticles
  • AI-assisted tools for XAFS sample design and absorption evaluation
